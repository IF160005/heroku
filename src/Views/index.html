About us

High quality flowers, exceptional service

Founded in 2016, Bouquets has already earned its name. Our brand is not only considered to be one of the most potential flower business nowadays, it is often also described as “unique” and “breathtaking”. We specialize in providing flower bouquets for any occasions, such as weddings, birthdays and any other. Flower bouquets range from elegant arrangements of roses, youthful yellow and white daisies to festive rainbows of tulips. The flower bouquet itself and its price depends on our customers’ needs.

Based in Kaunas, Lithuania.
